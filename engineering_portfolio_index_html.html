Industrial Butterfly Valve Redesign

CAD Redesign Mechanical Design

Redesigned a substandard butterfly valve CAD model into a more industrial-standard valve design using SolidWorks. The redesign focused on durability, sealing, manufacturability, and clearer functional operation.

  • Added a rubber/inner seal, improved connection points, simplified manufacturing, and created a motion study.
  • Designed around an approximately 3.85-inch valve diameter and pressure consideration near 27 psi / 300 lb.

FEA Analysis of Ducati Panigale Swingarm

FEA Mechanical Analysis

Analyzed the stress, deformation, and failure behavior of a Ducati Panigale V2 single-sided swingarm using simplified beam assumptions, representative dimensions, and material properties.

  • Studied rear-wheel reaction, acceleration, braking, cornering, bending, torsion, fatigue, and design alternatives.
  • Reported results included about 21.9 MPa von Mises stress, factor of safety around 6.6, and about 0.63 mm tip deflection.
FEA Load Analysis Von Mises Stress Fatigue Discussion
